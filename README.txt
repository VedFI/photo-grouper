PhotoGrouper v1.0 by VedFI

Group photos according to their date information from exif metadata.

USAGE
-Select source and destination directories. 
-Select the grouping mode. (copy or move photos)
-Analyze subdirectories in the source directory and  photo files in all these directories.
-Start grouping and wait until progress is complete.
